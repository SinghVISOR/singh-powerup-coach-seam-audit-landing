2. Digital Downloads — All Sales Final

2.1 Why this policy exists

Our digital downloads are delivered electronically and become accessible immediately on completion of purchase. Because the value of the product is delivered the moment you receive access, we operate an all-sales-final policy on digital downloads. This policy applies uniformly across SKUs, including (without limitation) the Fracture Diagnostic Toolkit (SKU #1), the Seam Maturity Model (SKU #2), all individual templates and domain toolkits, the Master Bundle, and any future digital SKU.

2.2 De-risk before you buy

To reduce the risk that a paid product is not a fit for you, we publish free assets that preview the methodology and the product structure, including:

  • the Fracture Self-Check at https://theseamlinemethod.com/self-check, a free interactive diagnostic; and
  • the Field Kit, a free PDF you can request at https://theseamlinemethod.com.

We strongly encourage prospective buyers to use one or both before purchasing a paid SKU.

2.3 Exceptions

Notwithstanding the all-sales-final rule in Section 2.1, we will issue a refund in the following circumstances:

  • Duplicate or unauthorized charges. Where a payment was charged in duplicate or was charged without the cardholder's authorization, we will issue a refund of the affected charge promptly upon verification.
  • Delivery failure. Where you completed a purchase but, due to a verifiable technical fault on our side, were not granted access to the digital product within a reasonable period and we were unable to deliver the product after a reasonable opportunity to cure.
  • Statutory rights. Where applicable law requires a refund that this Policy does not otherwise provide. See Section 6.

3. Self-Paced Courses

Self-paced courses are treated as digital downloads for the purpose of this Policy. Access is granted on payment and is non-refundable once granted, subject to the exceptions in Section 2.3.

4. Cohort Courses

Cohort courses are live, scheduled offerings with a fixed cohort start date.

  • Cancellations received in writing more than seven (7) days before the cohort start date are eligible for a full refund or, at your election, a transfer to a future cohort (subject to availability).
  • Cancellations received seven (7) days or fewer before the cohort start date, or after the cohort start date, are non-refundable.
  • We may, in our sole discretion, accommodate documented hardship by offering a deferral to a future cohort instead of a refund.

6. Statutory Rights (EU, UK, and Other Jurisdictions)

Nothing in this Refund Policy limits any non-waivable consumer right under applicable law.

6.1 EU and UK — Right of Withdrawal for Digital Content

If you are a consumer resident in the European Union or the United Kingdom, you ordinarily have a 14-day right to withdraw from a distance contract for digital content. However, that right does not apply where:

  • (a) performance of the contract has begun with your prior express consent; and
  • (b) you have acknowledged that you thereby lose your right of withdrawal once delivery has begun.

By completing a purchase of a digital download and clicking through to access the download, you expressly consent to the immediate performance of the contract and acknowledge that you thereby lose your right of withdrawal under EU and UK consumer law. The checkout flow records this consent.

6.2 Other jurisdictions

If your jurisdiction grants a non-waivable refund right not addressed above, that right applies notwithstanding any other provision of this Policy.
